How we work

Three rules we don't bend.

01  Native, all the way down

Our iOS apps are built with Swift and SwiftUI for the platform they live on. No web views pretending to be apps, no cross-platform compromise the user can feel.

02  Private by default

Your data stays on your device unless a feature clearly says otherwise. We don't show ads, and we don't sell data to anyone.

03  Small and sharp

Each app does one job exceptionally well. We would rather cut a feature than ship clutter.
