About

Software, built with intent.

Fintlock LLC is an independent software studio based in Florida. We design and ship software that makes complicated things feel simple enough to use without a manual.

Every product starts with the same question: what would this look like if it just worked? We hold that standard from the first line of Swift to the App Store listing.

Native, all the way down

Our iOS apps are built with Swift and SwiftUI for the platform they live on, not web views pretending to be apps.

Private by default

Your data stays on your device unless a feature clearly says otherwise. We don't show ads, and we don't sell data to anyone.

Small and sharp

Each app does one job exceptionally well. We'd rather cut a feature than ship clutter.
