The studio

Software, built with intent.

Fintlock LLC is an independent software studio based in Florida. We design and ship software that makes complicated things feel simple enough to use without a manual.

One standard, held end to end.

Every product starts with the same question: what would this look like if it just worked? We hold that standard from the first line of Swift to the App Store listing. Staying small is how we keep it. A small team can refuse the easy compromise, sit with a hard problem, and ship something that feels considered rather than assembled.

What we believe

Three rules we don't bend.

01  Native, all the way down

Our iOS apps are built with Swift and SwiftUI for the platform they live on, not web views pretending to be apps. The result is software that feels native because it is.

02  Private by default

Your data stays on your device unless a feature clearly says otherwise. We don't show ads, and we don't sell data to anyone. Privacy is the default state, not a setting.

03  Small and sharp

Each app does one job exceptionally well. We would rather cut a feature than ship clutter, because the things we leave out are what keep the product clear.
