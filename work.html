Fintley

Any address, instantly scored.

Buyers and investors drown in spreadsheets before they can judge a single listing. Fintley distills price, cash flow, market momentum, and location into one number from 1 to 100, then shows the full analysis behind it so the score is never a black box.

  • A single 1–100 score for any address
  • Price, cash flow, momentum, and location, weighed together
  • The full breakdown behind every number

A solo adventure that runs itself.

Tabletop role-playing asks for a group, a rulebook, and someone willing to run the table. Soloria removes all three. The app is the dungeon master, the rulebook, and the table at once, so a complete beginner can start an adventure tonight without scheduling anyone.
